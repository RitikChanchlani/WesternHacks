Who Are We?

We are a team of four post-secondary computer science students passionate about making a difference in the world. As technology becomes more and more advanced, our society has become as interconnected online as ever, especially in the middle of the COVID-19 pandemic. Growing up, we've all been taught the dangers of bullying, and how these risks have become even more prevalent online. With this in mind, our team decided to create a tool and scans text, protecting not only children, but members of society in general. On our team, we believe in the motto: "treat others the way you would like to be treated". For this reason, our unique algorithm analyzes text, either from user-input or from a website URL given by the user, and reminds the user of any possible negative interpretations of their messages before they may unintentionally spread hate around the world.

Our primary objective is to help encourage more positive language and communication, to make our world a better and happier place. Join us in accomplishing our mission!
